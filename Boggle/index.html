Don't know how to play Boggle?

Boggle is a really simple game to play.
Just hit the 'shake cubes' button and then letters will appear in the boxes.
Your goal to create as many words as possible just by using the letters provided to you.
Also, you can only go to the closest letter
that is next to a letter.
Easy right? Enjoy the game!
